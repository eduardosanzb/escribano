## Who we are

Escribano is operated by Eduardo Sánchez Bautista, Lübecker Strasse 15, 10559 Berlin, Germany (`info@escribano.work`). References to "we", "us", or "Escribano" in this policy refer to this sole operator.

## What the app collects

**Nothing.** The Escribano macOS app captures your screen locally and runs all analysis (VLM inference, LLM summarisation) entirely on your own machine. No screen data, frames, observations, or generated artifacts are transmitted to any server. Your work stays on your device.

## What this website collects

### Server access logs

Our web server records standard access log entries for each HTTP request. These entries include your IP address, the requested URL, the HTTP referrer, the date and time of the request, and your browser's User-Agent string. This processing is technically necessary to operate and secure the service. Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest). Access logs are retained for **14 days** and are then deleted automatically. No log data is shared with third parties.

### Analytics

This site uses **Umami Analytics** (self-hosted on European infrastructure at `analytics.raus.cloud`). For each page view, Umami transiently processes: the page URL, the HTTP referrer, the browser User-Agent string, and the approximate country derived from your IP address. **Your IP address is not stored.** No cookies are set, no cross-site tracking occurs, and no personal identifiers are retained or linked to you. Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in understanding aggregate site usage). No data is shared with third parties. Data is stored on European infrastructure only.

### No cookies

This site sets no tracking or advertising cookies. The only browser storage used is `localStorage` for your theme preference (light/dark/system). This constitutes technically necessary storage under § 25(2) Nr. 2 TTDSG — no consent is required, and this data never leaves your browser.

## Your rights under GDPR

You have the following rights regarding any personal data we process:

- **Access (Art. 15 GDPR)** — You may request confirmation of whether we process personal data about you and obtain a copy.
- **Rectification (Art. 16 GDPR)** — You may request correction of inaccurate personal data.
- **Erasure (Art. 17 GDPR)** — You may request deletion of your personal data where a legal basis for retention no longer exists.
- **Restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR)** — You may request that we limit how we process your data in certain circumstances.
- **Data portability (Art. 20 GDPR)** — Where processing is based on consent or contract and carried out by automated means, you may request your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.
- **Right to object (Art. 21 GDPR)** — You have the right to object at any time to processing of your personal data that is based on legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR), including any profiling based on that provision. To exercise this right, contact us at `info@escribano.work`. We will cease processing unless we can demonstrate compelling legitimate grounds that override your interests, rights, and freedoms, or for the establishment, exercise, or defence of legal claims.
- **Withdraw consent (Art. 7(3) GDPR)** — Where processing is based on your consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at `info@escribano.work`. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Berlin supervisory authority: **Berliner Beauftragte für Datenschutz und Informationsfreiheit**, Alt-Moabit 59-61, 10555 Berlin.
