## Who we are

Escribano is operated by Eduardo Sánchez Bautista, Lübecker Strasse 15, 10559 Berlin, Germany (`info@escribano.work`). References to "we", "us", or "Escribano" in this policy refer to this sole operator.

## What the app collects

**Nothing.** The Escribano macOS app captures your screen locally and runs all analysis (VLM inference, LLM summarisation) entirely on your own machine. No screen data, frames, observations, or generated artifacts are transmitted to any server. Your work stays on your device.

## What this website collects

### Email address (waitlist / contact)

If you submit your email address via a form on this site, it is processed by **Buttondown** (Buttondown, LLC, a US company) acting as our data processor. The legal basis is your explicit consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR). Data is transferred to the US under Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) as per Buttondown's Data Processing Agreement.

We use your email solely to send product updates and launch notifications. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email we send.

### Analytics

This site uses **Umami Analytics** (self-hosted on European infrastructure at `analytics.raus.cloud`). Umami collects anonymous page-view data with no cookies, no cross-site tracking, and no personal identifiers. No data is shared with third parties.

### No cookies

This site sets no tracking or advertising cookies. The only browser storage used is `localStorage` for your theme preference (light/dark/system). This data never leaves your browser.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Berlin supervisory authority: **Berliner Beauftragte für Datenschutz und Informationsfreiheit**, Alt-Moabit 59-61, 10555 Berlin.
