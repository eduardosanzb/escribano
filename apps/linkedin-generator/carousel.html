ADR-009: Always-On Screen Recorder
Accepted Revised
Status: Accepted (2026-03-12) — Phase A + Phase B validated
Context: Escribano processes pre-recorded video files. No way to "always capture" — user must remember to start/stop recording.
Decision:

Build an always-on screen capture process using Swift ScreenCaptureKit, managed as a macOS LaunchAgent, with three independent processes communicating through SQLite.
